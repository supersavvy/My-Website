about me

I'm a young and frivolous spirit trying to find her way in the world. I have many passions, some of which include: programming, dancing, eating, and teaching. I want to spend the rest of my life helping people and sharing my knowledge with my community, but for right now you can always find me at my local Pho shop, either preparing lesson plans or developing choreography.
